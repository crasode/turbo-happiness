Architectural Asphalt Systems

Common lines in BC include GAF and CertainTeed system packages with enhanced warranty tiers when full accessory requirements are met.

  • Limited lifetime base product warranty on qualifying shingle lines.
  • Enhanced options can extend non-prorated material periods to up to 50 years.
  • Program-dependent workmanship coverage can extend up to 25 years.

Impact and Wind-Focused Upgrades

For high-exposure coastal and hillside properties, accessory selection and fastening methods are selected to align with stronger wind warranty classes.

  • Wind limits vary by product and install method, often 110-130 mph ranges.
  • Some qualifying systems include no maximum wind speed limitations under specific programs.
  • Final warranty eligibility depends on full-system compliance and registration.

Flat and Low-Slope Membrane Systems

SBS and related membrane systems can include manufacturer material and system warranties with term options based on selected assembly and inspection requirements.

  • Typical term selections are 10, 15, 20, or 25 years on qualifying systems.
  • Coverage structure differs between material-only and full-system programs.
  • Written warranty scope is issued after final project registration and acceptance.

Warranty terms vary by product family and installation details. Final coverage is defined by manufacturer warranty documents issued for your completed system.
